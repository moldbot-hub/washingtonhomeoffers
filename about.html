About Us

Professional real estate solutions for Washington State homeowners.

Our Mission

Washington Home Offers was founded on a simple principle: homeowners deserve a fair, straightforward process when selling their property for cash.

We recognized that the real estate investment industry was full of opaque offers and high-pressure tactics. Homeowners facing difficult situations — foreclosure, divorce, inherited properties, code violations — were not getting the transparency they deserved.

We set out to change that. Our team combines deep real estate expertise with licensed general contracting experience, which means we know exactly what repairs cost and what homes sell for after renovation. We believe every seller should see those numbers before making a decision.

To date, our team has purchased over 500 homes across Washington State. Every single seller saw exactly how we calculated their offer before signing anything.

Our Approach

Most "we buy houses" companies make you an offer and expect you to take it on faith. We believe that approach is backwards.

Here is what sets Washington Home Offers apart:

  • We show you the comps. You will see 3-5 recently sold comparable properties in your area. These are the same comps we use to determine your home's after-repair value.
  • We show you the math. Our offer formula is straightforward: 70% of the after-repair value, minus estimated repair costs. No mystery, no black box.
  • We give you options. Cash is not the only path. We offer seller financing and novation agreements that can put more money in your pocket. We present all three options so you can choose what fits your situation.
  • We never pressure you. Receive your offer, take your time, consult your family or attorney. We are here when you are ready.

Construction Expertise

Our team includes licensed general contractors in Washington State. We handle renovations with our own crews, which keeps costs lower and quality higher. When we estimate repair costs for your property, those numbers are based on real construction experience, not guesswork.

This expertise also means we can purchase homes that other investors will not consider. Major structural issues, fire damage, water damage, code violations — these are problems our team knows how to solve. If your house has issues, we have likely handled worse.
